Frequent Asked Questions

Do I qualify for a cash loan?
Singapore Citizen, PR or Foreigner above 21 years old and permanently employed.
How much can I borrow?
Any amount for secured loans. For unsecured loans, $1500 to 6 times of your monthly income. T&C apply.
What are the interest rates?
The maximum rate of late interest a moneylender can charge is 4% per month for each month the loan is repaid late.
